About Me

I am Gaius Babarinde, a UX Researcher, Product Designer and Software Engineer. I currently live in Lagos, Nigeria, where i work as a Product Manager building awesome digital products by overseeing product life cycle.

I love transforming complex issues into simple user interfaces with a smooth experience for users. I build web, mobile and enterprise solutions.

The following highlights my strength, tools & technologies that i'm comfortable with:

  • Prototyping using Invision, Adobe XD and sometimes Figma.
  • Graphics Design with Adobe including Illustrator, InDesign and Photoshop.
  • Roadmapping & Planning with ProductPlan, Jira, SCRUM Framework, MoSCoW technique.
  • Greatly skilled in Communication, Attention to detail, Time Management, Team Leadership, Project Management.

My skills and expertise are in Product Roadmap Development, Agile Methodolgy, Product Research, Customer Analysis, Risk Management, Prototyping, A/B Testing, Data Analysis & Management, Product Marketing, Troubleshooting, Product Marketing, Training, Hardware Engineering, Software Development.
